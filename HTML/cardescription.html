BMW 5 Series (F10)

The BMW 5 Series (F10) represents the perfect blend of executive luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology. Produced between 2010 and 2017, the F10 generation brought a more refined, modern design with smooth lines and a confident presence on the road. Under the hood, the F10 offers a range of powerful and efficient engines—from the smooth inline-4s to the commanding turbocharged inline-6 and V8 options—delivering responsive acceleration and dynamic handling, true to BMW’s sporty heritage.
